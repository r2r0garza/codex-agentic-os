When no unblocked run is selectable, the worker sleeps exactly one `poll-interval` via the injected sleeper and clears `blocked_run_ids`, so a step that becomes eligible out of band (an operator `run approve`, or an earlier context step finishing) is retried automatically on the next cycle rather than being abandoned or busy-spun.
