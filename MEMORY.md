Also changed `start_next_step`/`transition_step` to derive the `step_started` history entry's `required_capability` from the step's own message rather than only from a resolved `ProviderRoute`, so a failed-routing dispatch still records the requested capability (`resolved_provider`/`resolved_model`/`routing_reason` stay `null`); fixed-provider steps are unaffected.
